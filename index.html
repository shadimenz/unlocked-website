Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unlocked — the couples card game app? ▼
Unlocked is an intimacy card game app designed for couples. It generates guided, AI-powered card experiences across five moods — Sweet, Flirty, Hot, Naughty, and Kamasutra — customized to your location, enhancements, and sexual orientation. Think of it as a date night game that fits in your pocket.
How is Unlocked different from other couples games? ▼
Unlike static card decks or generic couples quizzes, Unlocked generates unique card combinations every session using your location, preferences, and enhancements. No two nights are ever the same. It also includes Long Distance mode — a first-of-its-kind feature that lets couples play the same deck in real time from different cities.
What decks are available in the couples card game? ▼
Unlocked has five decks: Sweet (romantic & tender), Flirty (playful & teasing), Hot (sensual & explicit), Naughty (daring & adventurous), and Kamasutra (position-guided intimacy). Sweet and Flirty are free. Hot, Naughty, and Kamasutra require a premium subscription — with one free session to try each.
Is Unlocked a good date night app for couples at home? ▼
Absolutely. Unlocked is built for date nights at home. Pick a room — bedroom, living room, kitchen, bathroom — and the app tailors every card to your setting. Add enhancements like massage oil, restraints, or ice and the deck adapts to what you have available.
How does Long Distance mode work for couples? ▼
One partner creates a session and gets a 6-character code. The other enters it on their phone. Both devices show the same card simultaneously — one partner controls the pace, the other follows in real time. It's designed as a virtual date experience for long distance relationships, with cards written for two people playing from different locations.
What are the best virtual date ideas for long distance couples? ▼
Unlocked's Long Distance mode is one of the best virtual date ideas for long distance couples. You pick a virtual location — bedroom, hotel, sofa, or shower — and the app creates a shared scene for both of you. Cards escalate from tease to foreplay to climax, giving you a complete experience together even when you're apart.
Is Unlocked available for gay and lesbian couples? ▼
Yes. All decks — including Long Distance — fully support straight, gay, and lesbian couples. Cards use orientation-appropriate language, pronouns, and anatomy. Every relationship deserves intimacy tailored to them.
Is the app free? What does premium include? ▼
Sweet and Flirty decks are completely free with no account needed. Hot, Naughty, and Long Distance each include one free session. Premium unlocks unlimited access to all decks, all locations, and all enhancements — available as weekly, monthly, or yearly subscriptions.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Yes. No account is required to play. Long Distance sessions are temporary and automatically deleted after 24 hours. No personal data or session content is stored on our servers. Your intimate moments stay between you and your partner.
Is Unlocked available on iPhone (iOS)? ▼
Unlocked is currently available on Android via Google Play. The iOS version for iPhone is coming soon. Follow us or check back here to be notified when it launches on the App Store.
