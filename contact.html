Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect with my partner?

One partner creates a room and gets a 6-digit code. The other partner enters this code to join. Both devices will be synchronized.

Is my data private?

Yes! We don't require accounts or store personal information. Your intimate moments stay between you and your partner.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Google Play. Go to Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Unlocked → Cancel.

Can I request my data be deleted?

Yes! Email us at shadi1menzaljy@gmail.com with your request and we'll handle it promptly.
